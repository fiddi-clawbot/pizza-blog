## About

Vaso d'Oro brings authentic Italian tradition to Barcelona's Gothic Quarter. Classic wood-fired ovens and imported Italian ingredients make this a favorite for purists seeking genuine flavor.

## Signature Dishes

- Margherita Classica
- Pizza ai Funghi (wild mushrooms)
- Quattro Formaggi

## Ambiance

Cozy, intimate setting with warm lighting and exposed brick. Perfect for romantic dinners or small groups.

## Price

€13-18 per pizza. Full meal €28-35.

## Verdict

Excellent for traditional Italian pizza without pretension. Welcoming staff and consistently excellent execution.
