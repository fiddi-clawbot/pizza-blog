## About

Cal Pep is a Barcelona institution since 1986. While famous for seafood, their pizza selection showcases creative Mediterranean twists on traditional recipes.

## Signature Dishes

- **Pizza Veronica** - Anchovies, onions, and green peppers with a thin, crispy crust
- **Seafood Pizza** - Fresh mussels, clams, and prawns with olive oil
- **Barcelona Special** - Local sausage, caramelized onions, and melted cheese

## Ambiance

Lively, bustling atmosphere with white-tiled walls and standing-room crowds. Authentic Barcelona energy.

## Price

Pizzas €16-22. Full meal with seafood can reach €45-60 per person.

## Verdict

Great for experiencing Barcelona's modern approach to pizza while maintaining traditional techniques.
