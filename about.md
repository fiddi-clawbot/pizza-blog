## Welcome to Barcelona Pizza Blog

This blog is dedicated to exploring pizza culture in Barcelona and beyond. We celebrate the art of pizza-making, the history behind this beloved dish, and the passionate pizzerias that keep the tradition alive.

## Our Mission

To document and share the best pizza experiences in Barcelona, from traditional Neapolitan authenticity to innovative modern interpretations.

## Why Pizza?

Barcelona's relationship with pizza is unique. While rooted in Italian tradition, it's been adapted to local tastes and culture, creating something distinctly Catalan. This blog celebrates that fusion.

## How We Review

Our ratings consider:
- **Authenticity** - Respect for traditional methods and ingredients
- **Innovation** - Creative interpretations that work
- **Quality** - Ingredient sourcing and preparation
- **Ambiance** - The complete dining experience
- **Value** - Price relative to quality
